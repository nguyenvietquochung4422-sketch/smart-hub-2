About smART Hub

smART Hub is a Living Labs ecosystem — an intersection where the boldest ideas in technology, policy, and art are taken off the page to “live” and “breathe” in real-world contexts. It is where four streams — University – Government – Business – Community — converge to collaboratively decode complex urban challenges.
"On its journey to becoming a multidisciplinary and sustainable university, UEH does not stop at academic theories. The smART Hub Living Labs was established as a declaration of transformation."
